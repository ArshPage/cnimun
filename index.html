About CNIMUN'25

CNIMUN'25 is an immersive academic simulation that brings the United Nations to life by allowing students to step into the roles of diplomats. Participants engage in debates, negotiations, and problem-solving exercises, mirroring the real-world workings of the UN. This dynamic event provides a platform for students to explore international relations, develop critical thinking skills, and gain a deeper understanding of global governance.


The event places a strong emphasis on addressing pressing global issues, from climate change to human rights and international security. Delegates collaborate to draft resolutions, advocate for their assigned countries' positions, and work toward consensus. Through these activities, students learn the art of diplomacy, the importance of compromise, and the challenges of multilateral cooperation.


Beyond academic growth, CNIMUN'25 fosters leadership skills, public speaking confidence, and cultural exchange. Participants network with like-minded peers, build lasting connections, and leave with a heightened sense of global citizenship. By simulating the complexities of international diplomacy, the event empowers students to become informed, engaged, and proactive leaders of tomorrow

Letter from the Secretary General

"Some call it 'debate' I call it 'controlled chaos with parliamentary procedures'"

With years of experience and a deep passion for diplomacy, I am honored to serve as your Secretary General for the United Nations General Assembly at CNIMUN 2025. My journey in Model UN has been one of growth, learning, and fun but chaotic debates—quirks which I now hope to bring to this year's conference.

As your Secretary General, my goal is to ensure CNIMUN 2025 is a platform for intellectual exchange, spirited debate, and lasting connections. Whether you're a first-time delegate or a seasoned participant, I look forward to working with each of you to make this conference a rewarding experience.

Let's make CNIMUN 2025 memorable!
